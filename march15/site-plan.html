Home page

Welcome to our Thanksgiving celebration! As the year runs like weeks and months passing like days, we look forward to coming together with family and friends to give thanks for all the blessings in our lives. At our Thanksgiving celebration, we take pride in serving a delicious feast that brings people together. From well-cooked ills to pounding taro to sweet taro, our menu is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied and grateful. But Thanksgiving is more than just a meal. It's a time to reflect on the past year and express gratitude for the people and experiences that have enriched our lives. It's a time to gather with loved ones and create lasting memories that we'll treasure for years to come. Whether you're celebrating with family, friends, or neighbors, we invite you to join us for a warm and welcoming Thanksgiving celebration. From our table to yours, we wish you a happy and joyful Kapingamarangi Thanksgiving Holiday.

Kapingamarangi is a small atoll located in the Pacific Ocean, which is part of the Federated States of Micronesia. The people of Kapingamarangi celebrate Thanksgiving Day on the fifteen of March. Thanksgiving Day is an important holiday for the people of Kapingamarangi, as it is a time for giving thanks and expressing gratitude for the blessings in their lives. The day is typically celebrated with a community feast, where families come together to share a meal and give thanks for their food, health, and happiness.
